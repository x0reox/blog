This is a work-in-progress personal site/blog. Stay tuned for new posts.

I’m Ethan, a 23-year-old software developer currently living in central Arizona. I graduated in May 2025 with a B.S. in computer science from Loyola University Chicago. My technical interests include security, systems programming, and software infrastructure, which was sparked working on the SPHERE project at USC Information Sciences Institute. I was also involved in early research for an IEEE Access paper, PEARL, which applied large language model capabilities to effectively detect and explain hardware security vulnerabilities, including IC-embedded trojans. Outside of tech, I enjoy hiking, climbing, and basketball.
